About

This complete subject gateway is designed as a project work for Department of Library and Information Science, University of Delhi for the purpose of providing vast pool of information resources to every individual interested in this subject.

Its main focus is to provide complete access to various multimedia resources available on Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi (M. K. Gandhi), popularly known as Mahatma Gandhi covering various subject domains including a large collection of links to get more information on Gandhi's Non Violence, Peace and Conflict Resolution .
